How is the work on the project done?

1 Signing of the contract

After signing the contract, you can discuss the details of your future application directly with the project team. This will save time on the creation and approval of a technical specification. The discussion results in creating a detailed plan.

2 Technical specification

We develop technical specification based on the detailed plan. The process is also being agreed with you.

3 Interactive prototype

First, we design application screens and develop user interaction scenarios. Then we prepare an interactive prototype to show you the principles of your future application

4 Design

We work on the design options for the application. Then, based on your choice, we implement it in the design layout.

5 Development

After that, the development process begins: we program the work logic and the interface of your application.

6 Testing

We test your application on various devices using both, automated and manual testing. It helps to find and fix most of the bugs before publishing the application.

7 Deliver

Next, we upload your application on the main server.

8 Maintenance and support

We monitor your application's work for 1 months and, if necessary, make corrections for free. Then we either continue to support the application on a fee basis, or we pass all the necessary data to you.
